18. PROJECT PHILOSOPHY
===============================================================================

AQARION does not attempt to automatically discover mathematical truth.

AQARION measures how deterministic dynamics deform under observation.

Measurements generate evidence.

Evidence motivates conjectures.

Proof establishes mathematics.

Certification establishes reproducibility.

Formal verification establishes machine-checked correctness.

These remain separate at every stage.
